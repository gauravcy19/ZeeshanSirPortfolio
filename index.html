About Me

I'm currently Assistant Professor at Department of Computer Engineering, Jamia Millia Islamia (A Central University), New Delhi. He received M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Delhi Technological University, New Delhi and B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Uttar Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. He has more than nine years of teaching experience. His area of research interest is Code Mixing, Information Extraction and Retrieval, Text Mining, Natural Language Processing and Soft Computing Techniques.
